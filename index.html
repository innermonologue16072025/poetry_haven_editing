What is Poetry Haven?

Put simply, Poetry Haven is exactly what it claims to be: a safe haven for your poetry.

We know how precious these words can be so it is our goal to make you feel as comfortable as possible sharing your life's work with us. Privacy and confidentiality is our top priority, as we fully understand keeping a low profile.

It is our dream to help you bring your craft to life, so we offer detailed editing feedback whether you'd simply like a critique of your poem or manuscript (developmental editing) or if you'd like a detailed breakdown (line by line editing) with suggestions on where and how to improve. The goal of our editing is to make sure your words reflect who you are.

Whether it's typos, grammatical errors or you simply can't find the right words to articulate your feelings, that's where we come in. Sometimes you just need another pair of eyes, or two! We will both be proofreading your poems (for the price of one) included with all of our services, as well as the option to only choose proofreading. The choice is yours!

Our Team

Editor-In-Chief

Hi! I'm a.a.sunlitpoetry (She/Her). I'm from California, USA. I'm in my mid-twenties and I have been writing since childhood. The second I felt confident using a pen and forming words, I began to create the stories spinning around in my head and make them come to life on the page.

Age 13 is when I wrote my very first poem and fell in love with the craft because I was able to get my emotions on the page without so obviously spilling all of my secrets. Metaphors became my new language. Age 18, despite the pit in my stomach I took the leap and began my very first poetry account as @the_writing_hands in hopes one day I could really call myself a successful poet. Four years later, after a battle of pursuing poetry publicly or keeping it to myself I chose to start brand new as @a.a.sunlitpoetry. Seven years later, I have developed more than just a love for writing but a passion of reading and helping others find their voice through poetry.

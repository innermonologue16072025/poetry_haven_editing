Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the editing process take?

Our turnaround time depends on the service and length of your work. For individual poems: Light Touch takes 2-3 business days, Standard Edit takes 4-5 business days. For collections, we typically deliver within 7-10 business days. Rush services are available for an additional fee.

What types of poetry do you edit?

We edit all forms of poetry including free verse, sonnets, haiku, villanelles, sestinas, prose poetry, experimental forms, and more. Our editors specialize in various traditions and styles from around the world.

Do I retain rights to my poetry?

Absolutely. You retain all rights to your work. We provide a service to help improve your poetry, but the creative work remains entirely yours. We never claim ownership or rights to your poetry.

How do I submit my poems?

After placing your order through our contact form, you'll receive a secure upload link where you can submit your poems as Word documents, PDFs, or Google Docs. We also accept handwritten work through scanned images.

About Poetry Haven
